# Public legal and trust pages

## Pages added or updated

- `/privacy` - Privacy Policy
- `/terms` - Terms of Service
- `/cookies` - Cookie Policy
- `/contact` - Contact page
- `/about` - About page
- `/faq` - Frequently Asked Questions

## Purpose of each page

- **Privacy Policy**: explains what data is collected, why it is collected, where it is stored, third-party processing, GDPR-style user rights, and privacy contact paths.
- **Terms of Service**: explains core beta usage terms, account responsibility, feature volatility, future billing framing, and liability boundaries.
- **Cookie Policy**: explains essential and analytics cookie usage, cookie management controls, and consent behavior.
- **Contact**: provides a clear support channel for beta users and privacy-related requests.
- **About**: communicates the product mission and founder-led rationale focused on practical Dutch and A2 outcomes.
- **FAQ**: answers common pre-purchase and beta questions in a scannable format.
