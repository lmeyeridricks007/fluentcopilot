# FluentCopilot brand refresh

## Brand identity

- **Product name:** FluentCopilot
- **Capitalization:** always `FluentCopilot` (no spaces)
- **Domain:** [FluentCopilot.com](https://fluentcopilot.com)

## Positioning summary

FluentCopilot is a guided language-learning system focused on clear next steps, real-life communication, and measurable progress.

- Built first for Dutch learners in the Netherlands
- Structured for A2 and inburgering outcomes
- Designed to scale to additional language paths over time

## Messaging guardrails

- Keep the core brand language language-agnostic where possible.
- Use Dutch-specific framing in context (exam prep, Netherlands scenarios, A2/inburgering sections).
- Prefer phrasing like:
  - "Built first for Dutch in the Netherlands"
  - "Starting with Dutch, designed to scale"
